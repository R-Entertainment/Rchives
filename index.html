R LOGOS

featuring my self-created logos for my Facebook account ranging from various photo qualities
due to different editing softwares where I made them.




These were the first illustrated logos I've created through drawing with minor photo editing.
The first one features my four favorite games while the second one was inspired by the Disney Channel cartoon "Star Vs. The Forces of Evil".


The next two logos were created in Geometry Dash's Level Editor and just screenshotted making it not high quality, while the third one
was created in PixelLab, a decent mobile editing app.The left logo represents the baybayin equivalent of letter R, the middle logo was
created during the 2019 Senatorial Elections, while the last one is my 19th birthday logo with number 1 and 9 resembling the letter R.


These last set of logos were inspired by the yearly Christmas season and started from 2018. The first logo was created also in Geometry Dash's
Level Editor while the last two were in PixelLab. They all resemble a weird-looking tree because it also resemble a tilted letter R. The first one is for
Christmas 2018, the second one is for Christmas 2020 with COVID-19 pandemic, while the last one is for Christmas 2021 with the vaccination
program. A logo for Christmas 2019 was not made due to making a big project at that time.
